Manasi Vora is the VP of Strategy and Operations at Skynet Labs where she focuses on decentralized data storage. She is also the Founder of Women in Blockchain Boston with a mission to break down barriers to entry and increase gender diversity in the Blockchain industry. Prior to joining Skynet, Manasi conducted research at the MIT Media Lab's Digital Currency Initiative and was the Director of MIT Bitcoin Expo in 2019.
